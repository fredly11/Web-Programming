My Personal Interests

One of my biggest hobbies in gaming. I love video games and tabletop games of all sorts. I personally love games I can enjoy with my friends, or games that include a compelling story.

One of my life goals is to design games of my own, particularly video games. I want to use video games as a medium for telling some of the great stories I have developed in my life. As a part of this endeavor, I hope to express the imaginations in my head with a combination of writing, art and music that will sufficiently express them

In addition to games, I love reading, cinema, and learning new things. I enjoy creating armor and props for cosplay and LARP. I also love cooking, especially trying new, foreign recipes
